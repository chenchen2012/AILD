## Objective

Move from ad hoc AI drafting to a reliable content operating pipeline.

## Pipeline stages

- Strategy brief generation
- Channel-specific content drafts
- Brand and compliance QA
- Performance recap and prompt refinement

## Quality requirements

- explicit brand voice constraints
- fact and claim checks
- legal/compliance pre-publish review where required

## Metrics to track

- content production cycle time
- acceptance rate at first review
- campaign launch velocity
