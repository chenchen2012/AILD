How to Check AI Output Quality in Under 5 Minutes

Quality control is the bridge between AI speed and business trust.

The 5-point check

  1. Validity: Are claims factual and supported?
  2. Completeness: Does it answer the full request?
  3. Tone and fit: Is style aligned with brand and audience?
  4. Risk: Any legal, privacy, or policy concerns?
  5. Actionability: Can a teammate execute the next step immediately?

Fast review routine

  • Minute 1: Scan for factual red flags and missing context.
  • Minute 2: Check structure against required format.
  • Minute 3: Verify tone and business alignment.
  • Minute 4: Apply policy and risk checks.
  • Minute 5: Approve, revise, or reject with reason.

Where teams fail

  • No mandatory review owner
  • No policy reference point
  • No feedback loop into prompts

Fix these with the policy template and rollout cadence.
