The 30-Day AI Rollout Plan for SMB Teams

This guide is designed for managers who need measurable outcomes fast, not open-ended experimentation.

Week 1: Select high-friction workflows

  • Choose 1-3 recurring tasks with clear time cost.
  • Define baseline metrics: cycle time, error/rework, team effort.
  • Assign one accountable owner per workflow.

Use the selection criteria in 25 High-ROI Workflows.

Week 2: Install controls and standards

  • Apply policy boundaries from AI Policy Template.
  • Create a prompt scaffold and expected output format.
  • Define QA checks with 5-minute quality review.

Week 3: Team enablement

  • Train team leads on approved workflow usage.
  • Use one workflow daily with required QA signoff.
  • Capture failure patterns and update prompt instructions.

Week 4: Measure and scale

  • Compare post-rollout metrics to baseline.
  • Document gain drivers and residual risks.
  • Scale to next workflow only after stability checks.
