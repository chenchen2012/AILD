25 High-ROI AI Workflows for SMB Teams

Select workflows with high repetition, clear quality standards, and immediate business impact.

Operations workflows (10)

  • SOP drafts and revision summaries
  • Meeting transcript to action plan
  • Weekly KPI narrative generation
  • Vendor proposal comparison table
  • Project risk brief generation
  • Team handover note drafting
  • Status update normalization
  • Internal FAQ drafting
  • Process bottleneck analysis prompts
  • Executive pre-read condensation

Marketing workflows (8)

  • Campaign brief generation
  • Content repurposing by channel
  • Ad variation ideation with constraints
  • Persona-specific copy rewrites
  • Weekly performance recap drafting
  • SEO article outline generation
  • Email sequence draft generation
  • Landing page test hypothesis drafting

Support workflows (7)

  • Ticket triage support
  • Drafted first response templates
  • Escalation summary drafts
  • Knowledge base updates
  • Tone adaptation by customer tier
  • Resolution note standardization
  • Common issue trend briefs

How to choose your first workflow

Start with one item that appears daily and has a predictable output format. Then apply policy boundaries and QA checks before scaling.
